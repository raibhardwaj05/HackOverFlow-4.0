The Process

From Report to Repair

A seamless four-step lifecycle that connects citizens directly to repair crews in real time.

Step 01
🕳️

Spot the Damage

A cracked pothole or road hazard is identified by a citizen or smart sensor.

→
Step 02
📱

Instant Report

Snap a photo with the app — GPS pins the exact location and submits automatically.

→
Step 03
🗺️

Official Review

City dashboards surface the report with priority scoring and resource assignment.

→
Step 04
✅

Road Restored

Crew dispatched, repair logged, and citizen notified — green checkmark, done.

Advanced Capabilities

System Features

Every tool city officials and citizens need to keep roads safe and spending accountable.

🏠

AI Detection

Automated visual analysis to identify pothole dimensions and depth with high precision.

📈

Real-Time Tracking

Live dashboard for citizens and officials to track the status of every reported issue.

📍

GPS Reporting

Precise location tagging ensures maintenance crews find the exact damage site instantly.

👷

Contractor Assignment

Automated dispatch system for contractors based on location, expertise, and workload.

👁️

Transparency

Public-facing data on completion times and contractor performance ratings.

📡

Smart Monitoring

Continuous health monitoring of critical junctions and bridge infrastructure.
